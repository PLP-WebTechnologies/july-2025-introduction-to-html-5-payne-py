10 Simple Ways to Live More Sustainably

Published on August 1, 2025 by Jane Doe

1. Reduce Single-Use Plastics

Carry a reusable water bottle and shopping bag. Say no to plastic straws and utensils when possible.

2. Eat More Plant-Based Meals

Plant-based diets reduce environmental impact and are healthier. Try meatless Mondays to start.

3. Conserve Water

Fix leaks, use low-flow fixtures, and turn off the tap while brushing your teeth to save water daily.
